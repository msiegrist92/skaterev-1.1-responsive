31.75 x 14.25 x 8.125


6.75 Nose

6.5 Tail

Medium concave popsicle with shorter kicks. Similar shape nose and tail.

I set this board up on Indy 139s, 1/8 inch risers and 55mm wheels. Coming from a flatter board, it took some adjusting to the steeper kicks and concave of this board. For a steeper board, I'd set up smaller wheels and lose the risers. Once I got used to it, my pop felt good and the concave offered a great, but sometimes unpredictable flick.

One thing I liked about this board was how similar the kicks felt. While the nose is a bit longer and steeper, its not such a difference as other decks. With risers, manuals felt a bit harder and I'd get ghost pop skating the tail switch. DGK is made at Generator and their decks are always stiff and snappy.
