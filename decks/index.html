Deck Reviews

Most of a skater's deck selection comes down to personal preference. Due to this, decks are not scored like shoes are. The reviews here aim to provide concrete information about a deck's shape, including nose and tail length, shape, concave, and wheelbase. Tricks and obstacles that felt great to skate will be highlighted so that a skater can find the type of deck they like.
