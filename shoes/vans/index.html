Founded in 1966, Vans is one of the oldest companies currently making shoes for skateboarding. Vans is known for making one of the first shoes that was purpose-built for skateboarding. Vans has a heavy-hitting team and builds parks all across the world to host their contest series. Vans is owned by VF Corporation, who also owns Timberland, JanSport, North Face and Dickies.
