Shoe Reviews

Skate shoes are one of the best things about being a skateboarder. So many different styles, colors and brands to pick from, picking your next pair is a ton of fun. Here at skateREV our goal with shoe reviews is to provide an objective take on the performance of skate footwear. Shoes are measured on five different categories: Comfort, Flick, Board Feel, Durability and Protection. All categories have five possible points for a total of twenty five. We understand that comfort and flick may be subjective based on personal anatomy and the user's preference so we aim to be as transparent as possible with those particular scores.
