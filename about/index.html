skateREV was founded in 2020 with the goal of providing honest reviews of skateboard gear to the internet. There is currently little access to quality reviews on the internet outside of Mom's Zappos comments, or YouTube videos that may be too lengthy for some to sit through. Some of this information doesn't even come from skateboarders! We seek to create reviews of products that are both thorough and easily digestable.

Another issue we have noticed in the industry is that many products are reviewed by that company's sponsored athletes. Those videos are more like advertisements than a realistic take on the shoe for your average joe. skateREV is not aligned with any brand nor do we receive any free product. We spend our hard-earned money on our own gear just like you and aim to provide bias-free information.
